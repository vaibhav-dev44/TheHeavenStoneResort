About Our Resort

Nestled in the heart of scenic landscapes, The Heavenstone Resort offers an idyllic escape into nature's embrace. Our cozy villa resort features comfortable accommodation with lush green surroundings and breathtaking views.

Our elegantly designed villas boast modern amenities including air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and en-suite bathrooms for ultimate comfort.

Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, The Heavenstone Resort is your gateway to rejuvenation with its strategic location that allows you to explore the surroundings without having to travel far.
